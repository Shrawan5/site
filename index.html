About Me

I work at the intersection of storytelling, media, and social change. Trained in Journalism and Mass Communication, I use communication as a tool to connect, question, and inspire action—particularly within development and community contexts.

Photography has been a core part of my creative practice since 2020, allowing me to document moments that often go unnoticed. For me, images are evidence, memory, and emotion.

I’m deeply interested in how digital media and artificial intelligence are reshaping communication in Nepal and South Asia. I believe responsible storytelling can create understanding—and understanding can lead to change.
