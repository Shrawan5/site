About Me

Trained in Journalism and Mass Communication, I have worked as a Program Officer and Communication Officer with NGOs and community-based organizations, shaping narratives around education, youth engagement, and social awareness.

Photography has been a core part of my creative practice since 2020 — documenting people, places, and moments that often go unnoticed. My work blends aesthetics with responsibility.

I am deeply interested in digital media, artificial intelligence, and how emerging technologies can strengthen civic engagement — particularly in Nepal and South Asia.
